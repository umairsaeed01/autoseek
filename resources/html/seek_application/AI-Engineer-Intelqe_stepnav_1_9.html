About Us:
Atago engineering is an Enterprise AI and Test Data Engineering consultancy and solutions provider. Our flagship platform, IntelQE, is at the forefront of revolutionizing quality engineering through cutting-edge generative AI technologies. Our platform leverages advanced AI systems to optimize testing processes, predict defects, and enhance overall software quality. Join us to shape the future of quality engineering automation.

Role Overview:
We are seeking a highly skilled and experienced AI Engineer to lead the implementation of AI systems for predictive modelling and optimization in quality engineering for our enterprise clients. The candidate will work closely with our extended engineering team. The ideal candidate will have a strong background in AI, machine learning, statistics, knowledge engineering and cloud infrastructure, with expertise in generative AI orchestration frameworks such as LangChain and LangGraph.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Design and develop AI systems for predictive modeling of test cases, defect detection, and test requirement optimization algorithms.

  • Implement and optimize generative AI solutions using frameworks like LangChain and LangGraph.

  • Conduct research and development on innovative AI techniques, including knowledge engineering (knowledge graphs, ontology development).

  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to integrate AI models into the quality engineering automation platform.

  • Conduct research to stay updated on the latest advancements in AI and apply them to enhance the platform.

  • Develop scalable and efficient AI solutions leveraging cloud infrastructure (e.g., AWS, Azure, or Google Cloud).

  • Ensure the robustness, reliability, and performance of AI models in production environments.

  • Create and maintain comprehensive documentation for AI systems and processes.
